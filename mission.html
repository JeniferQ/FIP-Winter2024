How we started.

The change of plans

Our compromise with the environment started in 2001, when we felt the need to change our focus for a greater cause. Our company understood the importance of taking care of the environment, and so we decided to take action. With a deep sense of responsibility, we committed ourselves to incorporating eco-friendly practices into everything we do.

Every day, we're driven by our dedication to protecting the environment. We're always on the lookout for ways to use sustainable materials in our products and to make our manufacturing processes more energy-efficient. We're also proactive in reducing waste and finding innovative ways to reuse or recycle materials whenever we can. It's all part of our mission to lessen our impact on the Earth and leave a positive mark.

As we embark on this journey, we're thrilled to share our progress with you, our customers, and to inspire others to join us. We believe that by leading by example and being open about our sustainability efforts, we can create a community of people who share our commitment to the planet. Together, we're taking steps towards a greener, more sustainable future – and we're excited to have you with us every step of the way.

Our current goals.

For a better future

We're absolutely thrilled to share some exciting news with you: Dr. Nut is teaming up with One Planted Tree! Now, every time you purchase one of our products, you're not just getting something delicious – you're also helping to plant a tree in the Amazon rainforest. It's a partnership that fills us with pride, knowing that together, we're making a direct impact on one of the world's most precious ecosystems.

But that's not all! We're also excited to tell you that a portion of our profits goes to supporting the Amazon Conservation Team. They're on a mission to protect not just the environment, but also the cultures of indigenous communities living in the Amazon rainforest. By backing their efforts, we're not only safeguarding cultural heritage but also promoting biodiversity and sustainable living practices that benefit us all. Because preserving the unique cultures of the Amazon isn't just important locally – it's a global endeavor that offers valuable lessons in living in harmony with nature.
